Cigar

Cigar types

  1. Bottomshelf cigar $10-15
  2. Rocky patel $40-55

Cigar facts

  • Cigars are tightly rolled bundles of dried and fermented tobacco leaves, traditionally hand-rolled in countries like Cuba and the Dominican Republic.
  • The flavor and quality of a cigar depend on the origin and aging process of its tobacco, as well as the skill of its roller.
  • Unlike cigarettes, cigars are typically not inhaled, and their smoke is meant to be savored for its aroma and taste.
